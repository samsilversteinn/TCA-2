Whales!

Whales are large, intelligent, aquatic mammals. They move easily through water. They are the only mammals that live their entire lives in the water, and the only mammals that have adapted to life in the open oceans. Whales are the largest animals on earth. They range from 100 feet long and upwards of 200 tons.
